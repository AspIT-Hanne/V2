Shurima: The Desert Empire Reborn

Shurima is an arid, faded empire that was once the most powerful civilization in Runeterra, renowned for its **Ascended** (god-warriors created by the sun). Following the empire's collapse in a catastrophic event, the Shuriman desert lay in ruins for millennia. Its cities were covered in sand, and only nomads and dangerous creatures survived.

Azir's Resurrection

The empire was restored when the emperor **Azir** was resurrected and completed his **Ascension**. This restored Shurima's legendary Sun Disc, giving life back to the central oasis and the capital. Azir now seeks to reunite his people and rebuild the empire's grandeur, but he faces ancient threats, including the corrupted Ascended known as the **Darkin**.

Today, Shurima is a melting pot of nomadic cultures and Azir's rebuilt capital. It is a dangerous region, full of ruins containing magical artifacts, secrets of the Void, and fallen gods.

**Key Lore Themes:** Resurrection, Imperial Power, Legacy and Destiny, Ancient Corruption (Darkin).
