Piltover: The City of Progress

Piltover, also known as **The City of Progress**, is a flourishing, forward-thinking metropolis situated on the cliffs overlooking the sea. It is Runeterra's cultural and economic center, famous for its inventions, art, and trade. Piltover pioneered **Hextech**, a technology that combines magic with machinery, allowing anyone to harness arcane energy in a controlled manner.

Hextech Innovation and Class Divide

The city is governed by wealthy clans and the most brilliant minds, who compete to create the next great invention. This constant stream of innovation has led to a golden age of prosperity and security. But the wealth and purity Piltover enjoys is directly dependent on its counterpart below the cliffs: **Zaun**, where the industry and waste from Piltover create a toxic underworld.

Piltover Champions are often intellectuals, well-dressed, and reliant on their advanced Hextech gear.

**Key Lore Themes:** Innovation, Classism, Wealth, Hextech, Enlightenment.
