Targon: The Celestial Peak

Mount Targon is the tallest peak in all of Runeterra, a colossal, jagged mountain that pierces the atmosphere and connects directly to the **Celestial Realm**. It is not just a geographical location, but a nexus of cosmic power. Only the most determined and physically capable mortals attempt to climb the peak, and few return.

The Aspects

Those who successfully complete the ascent and are deemed worthy by the celestial entities may be chosen to become an **Aspect**. This process involves being imbued with the essence of a cosmic being, transforming the mortal into a near-immortal host for immense celestial power (e.g., the Aspect of War, the Aspect of the Sun). These Aspects are often used by the Celestials to enforce cosmic balance and fight global threats.

The communities that live around the base of the mountain are nomadic, hardy people who are highly spiritual and tied to ancient traditions of stargazing. Targon is a region of immense power, housing both divine champions and terrifying, god-like creatures.

**Key Lore Themes:** Cosmology, Destiny, Divine Power, Sacrifice, Celestial vs. Mortal.
