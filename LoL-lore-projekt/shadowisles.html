Shadow Isles: The Land of the Black Mist

The Shadow Isles were once the blessed **Blessed Isles** - a sacred land and guardian of magical knowledge. However, they were destroyed by a magical catastrophe known as **The Ruination**. A mad king attempted to revive his dead queen by misusing the sacred magic, leading to an explosion of corrupted energy. This energy instantly killed all inhabitants and bound their souls to the land in eternal torment.

The Black Mist and The Harrows

Now the islands are shrouded in a toxic fog called **The Black Mist**, which steals souls and creates undead beings. Every year on the anniversary of the Ruination, the Mist drifts across Runeterra during an event known as **The Harrows**, seeking to harvest more souls. The creatures that inhabit the islands are either powerful ghosts or monsters weighed down by the regrets of their former lives.

Central to the Shadow Isles is **Viego**, the Ruined King, whose regret and sorrow drive the Black Mist, and **Thresh**, the captivating jailer, who collects souls.

**Key Lore Themes:** Corruption, Grief, Undead, The Price of Forbidden Magic.
