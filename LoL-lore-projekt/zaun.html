Zaun: The Undercity of Chaos

Zaun is the grim, yet vibrant underworld located in the canyons below Piltover. It was originally part of Piltover but was shattered by a chemical reactor accident. Today, Zaun is covered in a toxic smog called **Zaunite Grey** and is home to those Piltover has rejected: unscrupulous inventors, anarchists, and street gangs.

Chemtech and Innovation from Below

In contrast to Piltover's sterile Hextech, Zaun's technology is **Chemtech** - raw, dangerous, and often mutating to those who use it. Despite the perilous conditions, Zaun is a place of wild innovation, where ideas deemed unethical in Piltover are freely explored. This has created a culture of survivors who value freedom and strength.

The central conflict is between **Piltover (wealth and order)** and **Zaun (poverty and freedom)**. Many Zaun Champions use chemical warfare, augmentations, or have been mutated by the city's toxic environment.

**Key Lore Themes:** Anarchy, Chemtech, Pollution, Social Inequality, Radical Freedom.
