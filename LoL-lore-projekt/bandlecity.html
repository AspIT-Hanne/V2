Bandle City: The Mystical Home of the Yordles

Bandle City is the ethereal, spiritual home of the **Yordles**—small, spirit beings that inhabit a realm partially connected to Runeterra. It is a place of endless wonder and subtle magic, often described as having "sunshine on the soul." The city itself is difficult for mortals to find, existing behind shimmering veils and mystical portals that appear sporadically across Runeterra.

The Yordle Veil

While Yordles can travel easily between Bandle City and Runeterra, the journey for mortals is dangerous and unpredictable, often leading to mental disorientation. The Yordles' primary characteristic is their ability to influence mortal perceptions, often manifesting as a sense of unexplained wonder, luck, or an intense desire to protect them.

Yordles who spend too much time outside of Bandle City risk losing touch with their spiritual home, causing them to develop more eccentric or rigid personalities, sometimes driven by a fierce need to prove themselves in the larger, mortal world.

**Key Lore Themes:** Spirit Realm, Whimsy, Innocence, Hidden Magic, The Nature of Perception.
