Runeterra: The World of League of Legends

Runeterra is the planet and world where all League of Legends lore takes place. The word "Runeterra" stems from the magical **Runes** that shaped the landscape and led to the cataclysmic **Rune Wars** that nearly destroyed civilization. Since then, the continent has been marked by strongly defined regions, each with their unique culture, politics, and magic.

The Major Conflicts

The world is constantly in conflict. The main global tensions include:

  • **The Balance of Power:** The struggle between Demacia's isolationism and Noxus's imperialism.
  • **The Magic:** The threat from the Void, the ancient Darkin gods (corrupted Ascended), and Lissandra's secret fight against The Frozen Watchers.
  • **The Technology:** The development of Hextech in Piltover and the polluted underworld of Zaun, creating new forms of power and inequality.

The entire continent is imbued with a natural magical energy. To understand the history of Runeterra is to understand how civilization has handled this wild and dangerous force.

**Key Lore Themes:** Magic, Power Struggles, Civilization vs. Nature, The Threat of the Void.
