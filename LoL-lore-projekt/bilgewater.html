Bilgewater: The Port of Lawlessness

Bilgewater is a chaotic port city built on the remains of giant sea monsters, making it a haven for smugglers, hunters, pirates, and those seeking to escape the law. It is a dangerous, unregulated place where wealth can be quickly gained and just as quickly lost. Here, there is no king or government; **power belongs to the one who can take it**.

Reavers, Sea Monsters, and the Shadow Isles Threat

The city is located in close proximity to the Shadow Isles, making it vulnerable to **The Black Mist** during The Harrows. To survive, Bilgewater's inhabitants hunt the monsters that inhabit the sea. Although pirates and warlords rule the streets, the city's true faith is tied to the ancient **Saltwater Scourge** spirits and the deep sea gods. They accept death as a natural part of life, which gives them a certain resilience against the Shadow Isles' eerie magic.

Bilgewater Champions are often rugged, ruthless survivors who use rusty weapons, gunpowder, and their own cunning to survive on the wild seas.

**Key Lore Themes:** Lawlessness, Survival, Piracy, Sea Monsters, Acceptance of Death.
