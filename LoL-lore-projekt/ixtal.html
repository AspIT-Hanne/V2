Ixtal: The Elemental Jungle

Ixtal is one of Runeterra's oldest civilizations, located deep within the jungles west of Shurima. Unlike other regions that suffered during the Rune Wars, **Ixtal deliberately isolated itself** from the outside world for millennia to protect its arcane technology and culture from external corruption. They consider themselves the last true inheritors of Runeterra's original magic.

Elemental Magic and Isolation

Ixtal's society is structured around **elemental magic**, which they consider the very essence of life. Their mightiest masters are called **Ixmendris**, and they are able to manipulate the elements to build and defend their remote cities, the most famous of which is **Ixaocan**. Their isolation is now threatened by Noxians attempting to penetrate their jungle, and their own curiosity about the world outside.

Ixtal's Champions are often fierce, elemental, and deeply protective of their home and traditions. They are experts at utilizing the jungle's natural defenses and magical energies.

**Key Lore Themes:** Isolationism, Elemental Control, Cultural Superiority, Ancient Civilizations.
