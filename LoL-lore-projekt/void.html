The Void: The End of Everything

The Void is a terrifying realm of non-existence that lies outside the known world of Runeterra. It is a primordial, alien place where the only desire is to consume and eradicate all life, emotion, and existence. The creatures that emerge from the Void—known as **Voidborn**—are monstrous mutations that follow a simple directive: **devour and grow**.

The Corruption of Icathia

The primary point of entry for the Void into Runeterra is the cursed land of **Icathia** (near Shurima). Icathia attempted to harness a forbidden power, which resulted in the land being ripped open, allowing the Void to spill forth. While the ancient Shuriman Ascended managed to seal the initial breach, pockets of the Void remain, and its malignant influence constantly leaks into the world.

The greatest threat from the Void is that it is not simply an enemy, but an antithesis to life itself. The strongest Voidborn Champions possess a frightening intelligence, and they represent the ultimate threat to the survival of Runeterra.

**Key Lore Themes:** Nihilism, Cosmic Horror, Consumption, The Ultimate Threat.
