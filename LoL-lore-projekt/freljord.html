Freljord: Land of Frost and Warbands

The Freljord is a brutal, icy wilderness where only the strongest survive. It is a place of raw, elemental magic and ancient traditions that date back to the time of the **Three Sisters**: **Avarosa, Serylda, and Lissandra**. The people are a hardy, tribal culture—descendants of primitive shamans and nomadic hunters—who survive by honoring the land and their powerful **Spirit Walkers** (shaman leaders).

The Warring Tribes

The Freljord is currently fractured, caught in a three-way power struggle, each tribe claiming the legacy of one of the Three Sisters:

  • **The Avarosan (Ashe):** Seek to unite the Freljord peacefully under the banner of Avarosa's ancient bloodline, focusing on rebuilding and stability.
  • **The Winter's Claw (Sejuani):** Believe in brutal conquest and the strength of the tribe, seeking to conquer the Freljord through military might and adherence to the old, harsh ways.
  • **The Frostguard (Lissandra):** Serve the hidden interests of Lissandra, the Ice Witch, who secretly guards the world against the **Frozen Watchers**—ancient, void-like entities sealed away beneath the ice.

The Freljord is also home to the **True Ice**, a magical substance that never dulls and is colder than any normal ice, often used to forge weapons. The greatest threat remains the deep, mysterious power frozen beneath the continent, hinted at by the mysterious, sentient **Brackern** crystal scorpions and the ancient legends of the Void.

**Key Lore Themes:** Unity vs. Division, Tradition vs. Survival, Elemental Magic, Ancient Threats.
